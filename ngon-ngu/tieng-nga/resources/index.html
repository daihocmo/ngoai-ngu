    Tiếng Nga¶

    Đọc hướng dẫn học của Refold để hiểu thêm về phương pháp học ngôn ngữ hiệu quả.

    Học Tiếng Nga¶

    Bảng Chữ Cái và Phát Âm¶

    • Blogs/Websites

      • Mimic Method: Master Class Russian: Fundamental Sounds, phonemes, and pronunciation awareness. Soundcloud for audio files.
      • Live Fluent: Russian Hard and Soft Consonants: Hard and Soft consonants guide.
      • Learn Russian with Kira: Video Course and companion book. Also a live masterclass that goes over the same content. More stuff via Patreon.

    • YouTube Videos

      • How to read Russian: Learn to read Russian cyrillic.
      • Russian Letters - Alphabet Song: Learn to read Russian cyrillic.
      • Soft and Hard consonants explained: Hard and Soft consonants guide.
      • Intro to mouth position and pronunciation of individual words and sounds: Phonology video.
      • Russian phonology playlist: Familiarize yourself with Russian phonology by watching this playlist (it has a few minor errors, but it's a good place to get some initial knowledge of the subject). Remember, you don't need to memorize all the rules or learn how to make all these sounds yet.

    • Bộ Thẻ Anki

      • Learn Russian Cyrillic and Sounds: A free Anki deck made by Refold to help you learn the sounds of the Cyrillic alphabet used in Russian, all with native audio.
      • Starter sentence deck made by Geert: Download Anki and the starter sentence deck made by Geert which also introduces the letters of the Russian alphabet.

    • Các Tài Nguyên Khác

      • Zero Beginner playlist: Start watching the Zero Beginner playlist from the Comprehensible Russian YouTube channel.
      • Master Russian: YouTube Videos.
      • Stage 0 and Stage 1 of the official roadmap: Read Stage 0 and Stage 1 of the official roadmap to learn the basic tools and ideas for learning a language with Refold.

    Từ Vựng¶

    • Danh Sách Từ Vựng Theo Tần Suất

      • Russian Frequency Lists: 1 - 5k words, categorically separated.

    • Bộ Thẻ Anki

      • Free Russian Decks: 1k to 10k Anki Decks RUS -> ENG, free download after signing up to the newsletter (scroll to the bottom).
      • 3000 most frequently used words Anki deck: Shared deck of 3000 most frequently used words. Each word comes with several example sentences. You can delete the "English to Russian" deck to focus solely on input.
      • RU15k 1T deck ordered by frequency: After you finish studying the Anki deck above, you can go for the community ru15k deck which contains 1T sentences with target words ordered by frequency.

    • Công Cụ Khác

      • Clozemaster: Fill in the blank flashcard system.

    Ngữ Pháp¶

    • Sách và Hướng Dẫn

      • Alpha Dictionary Russian Grammar Guide: A primer to help you understand Russian grammar.

    • Blogs/Websites

      • Learn Russian with RT: A traditional course by Russian state media.
      • Russian Cases: Russian case example.

    Từ Điển¶

    • Từ Điển Song Ngữ

      • English Wiktionary.
      • Reverso: Bilingual dictionary with corpus based examples.
      • Open Russian: Bilingual dictionary.

    • Công Cụ Từ Điển

      • Slava Popup Dictionary: A popup dictionary for Russian that even applies stress marks! Use this to do interactive immersion.
      • Golden Dict: Software for quickly looking things up in multiple dictionaries by highlighting text and pressing "ctrl+c+c". Russian and English Wiktionary are available. You can find more compatible dictionaries by Googling or on RuTracker.
